Free Support

Our goal at OpaVote is to provide you with the highest quality election services at affordable prices (many elections are free or only $10). To achieve our goals, OpaVote is completely self-service. Just about everything you need to know to run an election is available in our online documentation or can be determined by running a free election.

Where our documentation is unclear or incomplete or the unlikely event that OpaVote is not functioning correctly, we are happy to answer your questions.

To obtain free support from us, we ask that you do the following:

  • read our documentation,
  • run a test election,
  • ask a clear and specific question,
  • be respectful, and
  • use our support forum.

If you do, then we will happily answer you question. We frequently respond in less than an hour and otherwise in less than 24 hours. If you haven't done the above, then you may not hear back from us.

For questions about payments or sensitive information, send an email to team@whateveropavote.com.

Paid Support

If you need assistance beyond specific questions, we have paid support available for $50 for up to 30 minutes of assistance. With paid support, we can:

  • manually fix a mistake in your election,
  • help you understand the wide variety of available voting methods,
  • help you select a voting method that best meets the needs of your organization, and
  • possibly implement a custom feature.

Send an email to team@whateveropavote.com to request paid support.
